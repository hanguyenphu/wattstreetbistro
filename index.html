About

Welcome to Watt Street Bistro Restaurant

Located on 710 Watt Street in Winnipeg, Watt Street Bistro is one of Winnipeg’s top restaurants for the finest Vietnamese cuisine. Our menu features classic Vietnamese dishes such as beef and chicken noodle soup, rice vermicelli, and Vietnamese subs and buns. Whether you want to grab a quick bite to eat, relax after a hard day, or take the family out for dinner – our restaurant always packs the flavour. Join us soon and see for yourself! Start your meal with an authentic Vietnamese appetizer such as Fried Wontons, Charbroiled Beef Salad Roll (3 pcs), or the Vietnamese Spring Roll (6 pcs). For your main course, choose from a wide selection of rice vermicelli, soups, pad thai, or our sizzling steamed rice dishes! We recommend ordering the Sizzling Spicy Sauteed Shrimp and Onion. This spicy plate comes with sauteed shrimp and onions with steamed rice. Delicious! If that doesn’t entice you, grab a plate of the Deluxe Fried Rice, Beef Pad Thai Rice Noodle, and the Spicy Chicken with Lemon Grass and Peppers. If you are joining us for a quick bite to eat, order our delicious bite-sized dishes like the mac and cheese wedges, and chicken fingers. To complete your order, grab one of our tasty beverages like bubble tea or our authentic Vietnamese style coffee with condensed milk. No matter the occasion, Watt Street Bistro has something for even the most finicky diner. Take a look at our delicious menu and order online food delivery through SkipTheDishes and have your meal delivered straight to your door!
