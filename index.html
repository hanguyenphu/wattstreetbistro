About

Welcome to Watt Street Bistro Restaurant

Located on 710 Watt Street in Winnipeg, Watt Street Bistro is one of Winnipeg’s top restaurants for the finest Vietnamese cuisine. Our menu features classic Vietnamese dishes such as beef and chicken noodle soup, rice vermicelli, and Vietnamese subs and buns. Whether you want to grab a quick bite to eat, relax after a hard day, or take the family out for dinner – our restaurant always packs the flavour. Join us soon and see for yourself!
