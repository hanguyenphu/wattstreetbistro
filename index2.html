About

Thien Vietnam Saskatoon

With three locations in Saskatoon, Thien Vietnam is an award-winning restaurant specializing in Vietnamese, Thai, and Chinese cuisine.

Thien Vietnam’s mission is simple: to make healthy food at good prices. If you want mouth-watering Vietnamese, Thai and Chinese food without breaking the bank, come visit on of their three licensed dining rooms for breakfast, lunch, or dinner — open seven days a week. Thien Vietnam has legions of loyal diners, but there’s always room for more.
